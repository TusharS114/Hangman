Game Rules

Hangman Rules

Hangman is a word-guessing game where players try to discover a hidden word by suggesting letters. For each incorrect guess, a part of a hangman is drawn. The goal is to guess the word before the hangman is fully drawn.

Play Hangman

Number Guess Rules

Number Guess is a game where players try to guess a randomly generated number within a specified range. Hints are provided to guide players in adjusting their guesses. The objective is to guess the correct number.

Play Number Guess

Tic Tac Toe Rules

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game where symbols (X and O) are placed on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols. The game ends in a draw if the grid is filled without a winner.
